Carebuddy is a wearable device that strives to improve the health of both patients and their caregivers, specifically for seniors who are cared by their family members. It utilizes technology like GPS, RFID, fall sensor, and fitbit-like activity tracker that can be customized to actively prevent patients from harm.

Care Buddy aims to give caregivers’ personal lives back, and give patients a sense of freedom in their own homes, while receiving the same level of care from their caregivers, if not higher.

Why do dementia patients wander?

Often, dementia patients feel overwhelmed due to environmental factors like loud noises.

Dementia patients would go through the house looking for what they need, and sometimes end up wandering out of the house.

Dementia patient sometimes leave the house to perform past daily routines in their memory such as picking children up from the school or going to church.

System Features

Alert Levels – the system categorizes different situations that would trigger alerts into three different levels.

Location Sensitive – Same trigger action in different locations triggers different levels of alerts.

Customization – Alerts can be temporarily diable based on the schedule to accomandate every care recipients' needs.

Incremental Alerts – System would upgrade alerts if the trigger action persists.

User Interface

The interface aims not to be cognitively overwhelming for the caregivers. It uses different colors to indicate care recipient’s status. It also utilizes large icons and text for easy navigation.

Customization

Caregiver can set up ideal heart rate for the patient and activities such as exercise and medication that would affect the heart rate.

Caregiver can define hazardous area, night area and the distance allowed for the patient to be away from the house.

Caregiver can schedule activities that would potentially trigger false alarms such as exercise, or hospital trips.

Caregiver can decide if the system would contact emergency personnel or simply send an alert to the caregiver in certain urgent situations.
