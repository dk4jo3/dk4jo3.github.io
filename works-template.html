How might we help users to understand more about their utility consumption? This project explored the possibilities of utilizing Internet of Things devices to assist homeowners to manage and be concious of their energy consumption.
I contributed the most of my time being in charge of the animation, gestural control, majority of user interface as well as interviews.

Target User

We started off narrowing down the target user groups of this application and created persona to imagine the different ways and reasons that they would possibly use the application.

We decided to focus on the ones who would benefit from this application the most and created experience map to understand their behavior as well as their wants and needs.
