CM² Investment Platform

A Crowd-Sourced Investment Platform Powered by Blockchain

I designed and developed CM Square Investment Platform with the focus of creating a playful yet user-centric experience that makes real-estate investing more approachable.

Concept

Investors can purchase a portion of the property with CM2 Token (CM2T). They can also trade CM2T on several cryptocurrency exchanges.

The current scope entails proof of mobile and desktop concept with CM Square's property listings and fixed pricing. In the future, property listings from other companies will populate the platform, and the supply and demand will determine the prices.

Sketches

There are three main pages: market (property listings, and property details), portfolio (properties owned), wallet (CM2T balance and transaction)).
